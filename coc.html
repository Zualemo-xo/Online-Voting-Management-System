    Code of Conduct

A never ending journey towards hassle-free elections

Register Your Valuable Vote!

VOTE!

Register your vote well before the polling time finishes.ECI cannot take responsibility for non-registrations due to last minute traffic.

BE PREPARED!

Please have your UVID in hand. Do not search for it, only when the polling day comes.

HELP ELDERS!

The world is a better place when people unite to fight against the all mighty evil. Teach the elders about how to use the voting wesbite.

Right Not to Vote (NOTA)!

Voters have been granted the right not to vote, which is recorded in the system. Also known as a NOTA (None of the above) vote.
